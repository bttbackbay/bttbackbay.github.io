Meet Your Instructors

Professor Rafael Costa

3rd Degree Black Belt • Head Instructor & Founder

Over 15 years of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu experience with multiple regional and national competition titles. Dedicated to creating a welcoming, technical training environment for students of all levels.

Coach Marcus Kim

Black Belt • Adults Program Director

Specializes in modern competition techniques and No-Gi grappling. Known for breaking down complex movements into digestible steps. Passionate about helping adult beginners build confidence through Jiu-Jitsu.

Coach Sarah Martinez

Brown Belt • Kids Program Lead

Has been teaching martial arts to children for over 8 years. Focuses on building discipline, focus, and self-esteem through age-appropriate BJJ instruction. Creates a fun, safe environment where kids learn valuable life skills.
